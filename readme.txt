With this plugin you can let your website visitors rate single pages, posts or custom post types with a simple rating tool.

== Description ==

Ever wanted to know, how your single sites are rated by your actual website users? With Simple Site Rating you can give your visitors an easy but effective tool to rate all your posts and pages just with a single click. All statistics will be shown to you within the WordPress admin panel.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/wsm-page-rating` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Change your rating settings located at 'Settings > Simple Site Rating' like activating desired post types, customizing colors etc.
3. Place the shortcode `[simple-site-rating]` within your page (e.g. in the page footer or with a shortcode block in a widget).
4. Find all ratings in overviews within wp-admin (e.g. 'Pages' or 'Articles' overview).
